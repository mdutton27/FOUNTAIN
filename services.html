Services

Research-led approaches tailored to your organisation's needs. Every engagement is designed to deliver actionable insights.

Innovation Workshops

Harness the power of group energy to generate breakthrough ideas, align stakeholders, and transform insights into actionable innovation opportunities. Our facilitated workshops bring together diverse perspectives to solve complex challenges and drive meaningful change.

Workshop formats:

  • Design sprints and rapid prototyping
  • Ideation and brainstorming sessions
  • Co-design workshops with users
  • Future visioning and scenario planning
  • Problem framing and opportunity mapping
  • Stakeholder alignment sessions

Outcomes:

  • Prioritised innovation opportunities
  • Rapid concept prototypes
  • Aligned stakeholder vision
  • Actionable next steps and roadmaps

User Research

Comprehensive research programmes that reveal what your users truly need, think, and feel. We employ a range of qualitative and quantitative methods to build a complete picture of your user landscape.

Methods include:

  • In-depth user interviews
  • Survey design and analysis
  • Usability testing (moderated and unmoderated)
  • Diary studies
  • Focus groups and workshops
  • Persona and journey mapping
  • Competitive analysis

Deliverables:

  • Research reports with actionable recommendations
  • User personas and archetypes
  • Journey maps and experience blueprints
  • Video highlights and insight summaries
  • Stakeholder presentations

Rapid Prototyping

Leverage AI-powered tools like Lovable and Claude to build functional prototypes with production-ready design libraries. Move from concept to clickable prototype in days, not weeks, enabling faster validation and stakeholder alignment.

Our approach:

  • AI-assisted design and development
  • Production-ready component libraries
  • Rapid iteration cycles
  • Interactive, clickable prototypes
  • Design system integration
  • Developer handoff documentation

Benefits:

  • Validate concepts before heavy investment
  • Accelerate stakeholder buy-in
  • Reduce development rework
  • Test with real users earlier

Interaction Design

Craft intuitive, delightful user experiences through thoughtful interaction patterns, micro-interactions, and seamless user flows. We design interfaces that feel natural and guide users effortlessly toward their goals.

What we design:

  • User flows and task flows
  • Wireframes and interface layouts
  • Micro-interactions and animations
  • Responsive and adaptive designs
  • Accessibility-first interactions
  • Design system components

Outcomes:

  • Reduced user friction and errors
  • Increased task completion rates
  • Consistent, scalable design patterns
  • Delightful user experiences

Contextual Enquiry

Observe and interview users in their natural work environment to understand how they actually use products and services. This method reveals workflows, workarounds, and pain points that users often cannot articulate in interviews.

Process:

  • On-site observation sessions
  • Concurrent think-aloud protocols
  • Task analysis and workflow mapping
  • Environment and context documentation
  • Affinity diagramming and synthesis

Benefits:

  • Uncover hidden user needs and behaviours
  • Identify efficiency improvements
  • Understand the full context of use
  • Ground design decisions in real-world evidence

Information Architecture

Structure your content and navigation so users can find what they need intuitively. Good information architecture is invisible to users - they simply find what they're looking for without friction.

Methods:

  • Card sorting (open and closed)
  • Tree testing
  • Content audits and inventories
  • Taxonomy development
  • Navigation design and testing
  • Site mapping and content modelling

Outcomes:

  • Improved findability and task completion
  • Reduced support queries
  • Scalable content structures
  • Evidence-based navigation design

Experience Strategy

Align your product and service experiences with user needs and business objectives. We help organisations develop research-informed strategies that guide design and development decisions.

Services:

  • UX maturity assessment
  • Research programme design
  • Roadmap prioritisation (Kano, MoSCoW, Value vs Complexity)
  • Design thinking facilitation
  • Stakeholder alignment workshops
  • Capability uplift and mentoring

Usability Testing

Validate designs with real users before development, or identify issues in existing products. We conduct rigorous usability testing that delivers clear, prioritised recommendations.

Formats:

  • Moderated lab-based testing
  • Remote moderated sessions
  • Unmoderated remote testing
  • Guerrilla testing
  • A/B testing analysis
  • Heuristic and expert reviews

Deliverables:

  • Prioritised findings and recommendations
  • Video highlights
  • Severity ratings and impact assessment
  • Design recommendations
